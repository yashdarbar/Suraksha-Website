Immediate Help at Your Fingertips

Here are a few reasons why our users choose the Suraksha app.

Real-time Assistance

Stay Protected 24/7 with Suraksha, the app designed to address the safety concerns of women in India. With the increasing number of incidents of violence against women, it has become crucial to have an app that can provide immediate help. Suraksha offers real-time assistance, ensuring that help is just a tap away, no matter the time of day. Trust our app to provide round-the-clock support and immediate assistance during emergencies.

Location Tracking and Sharing

One of Suraksha's key features is its ability to track and share your location. By enabling location tracking on Suraksha, you can share your precise location with your emergency contacts, ensuring that help reaches you quickly. Even if you're unable to provide directions, Suraksha's location sharing feature guides emergency responders to your exact location, helping you find your way to safety. Empower yourself with the ability to share your location and let your loved ones know where you are in case of an emergency.

Customizable Emergency Contacts

Suraksha also offers customizable emergency contacts, allowing you to select your trusted circle. You have the freedom to add family members, friends, or trusted contacts as emergency contacts on Suraksha. By customizing your emergency contact list, you ensure that the right people are notified in times of distress. Reach out to your support system with confidence, knowing that Suraksha puts you in control of your safety.

Multi-platform Compatibility

Experience the convenience of Suraksha on all your devices. Whether you're using a smartphone, tablet, or wearable device, Suraksha is compatible across multiple platforms. Seamlessly integrating with your preferred operating system, Suraksha ensures a smooth user experience wherever you are. Accessible anywhere, anytime, Suraksha provides a consistent level of safety and protection across all your devices. magna.

User Community and Support

Join the supportive community of Suraksha users and be part of a network that promotes safety. Share experiences, seek advice, and support each other within the Suraksha user community. In case you have any queries or concerns, our dedicated customer support team is always ready to assist you. Together, we can create a safer environment for all women in India by using Suraksha and strengthening the network of support.

Enhanced Privacy and Security

Privacy and security are paramount when it comes to Suraksha. Your safety is our top priority, and we ensure that your personal information is protected at all times. Suraksha employs secure data encryption, ensuring that your data is safely stored and only accessible to you. Trust in Suraksha's robust security measures, knowing that we prioritize your privacy and maintain the confidentiality of your information.
